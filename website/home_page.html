How to Use the System

  • Welcome to the homepage of the gait recognition system! Users are encouraged to upload a short video clip of a person walking, with the full body visible.
  • The system is designed to identify a unique walking pattern among all individuals and extract gait features.
  • The system will match the gait features with stored data for human identification and verification.
  • Videos of an mp4 format are acceptable to upload.
